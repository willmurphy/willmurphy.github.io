An 18-Minute Backdoor Exposes the AI Developer Stack

A trojanized build of the Nx Console VS Code extension — a tool with millions of installs and verified-publisher status — was live on the marketplace for just 18 minutes on May 18, yet the group claiming responsibility, TeamPCP, used it to exfiltrate roughly 3,800 internal GitHub repositories and a trove of cloud, CI/CD and AI-assistant secrets. Researchers note the malware specifically searched for AI coding-assistant configuration files, an early sign that attackers now treat the AI dev layer as a target. The breach traced back to a separate compromise of the TanStack npm package, underscoring how supply-chain risk cascades.
