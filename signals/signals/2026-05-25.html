Cannes Bars Generative AI From the Palme d'Or

The 79th Festival de Cannes formally ruled that films in which generative AI drives scripting, visual generation or principal-performance synthesis are ineligible for the Official Competition, while technical uses such as restoration, image cleanup and VFX on human-shot footage remain permitted.
