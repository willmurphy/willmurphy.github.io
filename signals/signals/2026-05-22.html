Signals: Microsoft Agent 365 Generally Available · Google's 24/7 Gemini Spark Agent · MCP Security Standards Emerge · Realtime Voice Models Ship In The API

The headline-grabbing model launches have given way to a quieter, more consequential shift: autonomous agents moving from preview into generally available products that run continuously and act on a user's behalf. The structural change is less about smarter answers than about software that does things while you sleep.

The Great Social Exit And The Pull Of The Local

Signals: Time On Social Forecast To Fall In 2026 · "Everything Local Again" Re-Anchoring · Authenticity Over Performance · Gen Z & Alpha Shift From Scrolling To Shaping

Two cultural currents are reinforcing each other: a measurable "social exit," with time on the big platforms forecast to drop as newsletters, podcasts, and reading rituals reclaim status, and a broad "re-anchoring" toward tradition, locality, and rootedness from the US to India to the Gulf. After a decade in which the algorithm flattened culture into one global feed, consumer psychology is pulling identity back toward the specific and the human. Owned, lower-frequency channels — newsletters, communities, and place-based experiences — are where attention is migrating; brands and creators that build there now will own relationships the platforms can no longer rent them.
