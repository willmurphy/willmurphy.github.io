The Post-Digital Recalibration Is A Real Wedge

Signals: Pew Finds 1 In 5 US Teens On TikTok/YouTube "Almost Constantly" · 41% Of Gen Z Use Social As Primary Search · 41% Of Americans Actively Cutting Screen Time · Mastercard Reports Run Clubs And Niche Gatherings Surge · 64% Of Consumers Say Social Media Harms Kids

Two contradictory data sets are running in parallel: deep digital embedment (chatbots, social-first information habits, parasocial creator economies) and a visible, paying counter-movement back to physical presence and "re-anchored" tradition. This is not a return to 2014; it is the same cohort optimizing for both poles simultaneously and treating each as a status signal.

Top Opportunity

Brands and products that bridge — in-person experiences with digital-native distribution, or AI tools that explicitly protect attention rather than capture it — own the next eighteen months. Pure-attention business models are about to face their first generationally hostile audience.
