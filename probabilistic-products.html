Probabilistic Products

The next big paradigm shift in software is already here. Probabilistic Products explores the unique design, product management, and development challenges of building AI-driven software.

Unlike deterministic software that always does exactly what you code it to do, AI products behave probabilistically—they make educated guesses, learn from data, and sometimes make mistakes.
