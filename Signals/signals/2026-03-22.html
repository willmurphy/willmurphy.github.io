February Jobs Report: -92,000 Positions, Unemployment at 4.4%

The US economy shed 92,000 nonfarm payroll jobs in February — the third monthly decline in five months — well below the already-grim consensus estimate of -50,000. The unemployment rate rose to 4.4% and the labor force participation rate fell to 62%, its lowest since late 2021. Long-term unemployment averaged 25.7 weeks, the longest since December 2021. Despite the headline weakness, average hourly earnings grew 3.8% year-over-year, complicating the inflation picture.
