Will Murphy

Hi, I'm Will. I like to build the future. I also have a passion for making teams more innovative. I'm a Principal Product Manager in the AI Innovation group at Intuit (Futures Group). I previously led AI new product development at Data.ai (formerly App Annie).

I'm an OnDeck Fellow (Founder Fellow and Corporate Innovation Fellow). I founded MessagePath, a top 5 AI Writing Assistant Software (G2.com). Before that, I was founding Head of Product at Talla, an enterprise AI chatbot startup that was acquired. Prior to that, I was a co-founder of SenseAware, a pioneering IoT analytics startup for FedEx. I led early-stage bets at the FedEx New Product Innovation Labs, the company’s former emerging tech and new product lab. (We had a good run.) While there I spearheaded data-driven commerce opportunities and pitched the concept that became FedEx DataWorks, FedEx's data science division. I also researched a lot of things like drone delivery and set up a partnership with eBay to create a new joint service. I also founded the EPICenter Logistics Innovation Accelerator (startup accelerator), sponsored by FedEx. In my free time, I founded, built, launched, and sold SurfRate, a web search engine based on user reviews. I hold multiple patents in AI, intelligent agents, online ads, e-commerce, data management, knowledge graphs, and IoT.

I'm an occasional guest speaker at venues such as Stanford University, MIT VLAB, the Global Artificial Intelligence Conference, The AI Conference, General Assembly, the World Crypto Economic Forum, and SVI Academy, where I speak about AI and innovation.
