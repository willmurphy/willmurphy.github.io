Will Murphy

Hi, I'm Will Murphy. I obsess over strategy, product, and design and I have a passion for making groups of any size more innovative. I'm the creator of the concept of the "probabilistic product", the new set of design patterns for designing AI-driven products.

I previously led early-stage AI bets at Data.ai (formerly App Annie), bringing many of them to market. I'm an OnDeck Fellow (Founder Fellow and Corporate Innovation Fellow). I founded MessagePath, a top 5 AI Writing Assistant Software (G2.com). Before that, I was founding Head of Product at Talla, an enterprise AI chatbot startup. Prior to that, I was a co-founder of SenseAware, a pioneering IoT analytics startup for FedEx. In my free time, I founded, built, launched, and sold SurfRate, a pioneering web search engine based on user reviews. I led early-stage bets at the FedEx New Product Innovation Labs, the company’s former emerging tech and new product lab. (We had a good run.) While there I spearheaded data-driven opportunities and pitched the concept that became FedEx DataWorks, FedEx's data science division. I also founded the EPICenter Logistics Innovation Accelerator (startup accelerator), sponsored by FedEx. I hold multiple patents in AI, intelligent agents, online ads, e-commerce, data management, knowledge graphs, and IoT.

I'm an occasional guest speaker at venues such as Stanford University, MIT VLAB, the Global Artificial Intelligence Conference, The AI Conference, General Assembly, the World Crypto Economic Forum, and SVI Academy, where I speak about AI and innovation.
