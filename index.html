Background

About Me

Thanks for visiting. This page is partially an "about me" and mostly a place where I share some things I've worked on that others might find useful. I'm a Silicon Valleytech and product leader who has spent over a decade at the intersection of AI, product strategy, and zero-to-one innovation (Startups & Corporate Labs). At Intuit's Futures Group, I launched two AI agents, then led the charge to build Intuit's next-generation flagship agent platform using neuro-symbolic AI and small language models. Before that, I led AI new product development at Data.ai Labs (formerly App Annie).

I founded MessagePath, a top-5 AI Writing Assistant on G2.com, and was the founding Head of Product at Talla, an enterprise AI chatbot startup that was acquired. Earlier, I co-founded SenseAware, a pioneering IoT analytics venture for FedEx, and led FedEx Innovation/ new product missions where I created a partnership and co-developed a service with eBay. I also founded the EPICenter Logistics Innovation Startup Accelerator and pitched/ spearheaded the concept that became FedEx DataWorks. My idea was to launch a new FedEx division that would utilize FedeEx's vast dataset to launch new, high-margin, data-driven services.

I pioneered the concept of the "probabilistic product" in 2016 to describe the unique design and product management challenges of AI-driven software. I'm an OnDeck Fellow (Founder & Corporate Innovation Fellow) and hold multiple patents in AI, intelligent agents, knowledge graphs, and IoT. I speak on AI and innovation at venues including Stanford, MIT VLAB, and the Global AI Conference.
