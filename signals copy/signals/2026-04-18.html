The cohort that built modern platform culture is now leading the retreat from it. The move is not anti-digital — it is anti-feed: book clubs, walking groups, creative workshops, and intentionally small group chats are replacing the infinite scroll as the primary social unit. Brands and media built around broadcast-scale feeds should stand up a second playbook for niche, place-based, low-audience formats — sponsor the book club, host the run, fund the workshop. Treat audience depth, not reach, as the KPI for the next 18 months.
